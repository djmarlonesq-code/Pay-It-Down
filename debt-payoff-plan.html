How to Use This Plan
▾
1

Find your current paycheck period

Tap Pay 01 to expand it. Each card shows exactly how much to pay that period. The FOCUS card gets the bulk of your $650. All others get their minimum payment only.

2

Make your payments

Pay each card the amount shown. The FOCUS card is the priority — pay that first. MIN payments keep other cards current and avoid late fees. PAID OFF means that card hits zero this paycheck.

3

Recording a payment you already made

Open Balances, Limits & APRs above. Find the card and enter the amount already paid in Extra Paid ($). Hit Recalculate Plan and the schedule updates with your reduced balance.

4

Updating balances after a statement

After your statement closes, open the editor and update Balance ($) to your current statement balance. Hit Recalculate — the entire payoff timeline adjusts instantly.

5

Paying extra one paycheck

Have extra cash? Bump up Budget per Paycheck temporarily and recalculate, then set it back next time. Or enter the extra in Extra Paid on your FOCUS card — same result.

6

Understanding the pill labels

FOCUS — max payment this period.  MIN — minimum only, keeping it current.  PAID OFF — card hits zero this paycheck. Freed budget rolls automatically to the next card in line.

* Interest estimated using daily periodic rate x 14 days applied to opening balance each period.
